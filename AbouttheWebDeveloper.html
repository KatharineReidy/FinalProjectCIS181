About The Web Developer

The website is a project for my Web Development Class. I am new to web development and this is the first website I have ever attempted to create. The assignment asked for us to create a website about a place we would like to visit. I like visit the parks near my home. I also see many tourists in my neighborhood. So, I decided to create a website that could be useful to people who visit my neighborhood.

I shot and edited all photography & video on this website. I used Canva to create & edit the imagery for this website.
