About Katharine

Hello, My name is Katharine and this website is a project for a Web Development class. I am extremely new to Web Development and this is the very first web site I have ever attempted to develop. I am studying Digital Marketing and Social Media Management at Harold Washington College. I an older student, returning to school after decades of work and life experiences.

I chose Grant Park, the Chicago Lakefront, and the Chicago Riverwalk as the topic of my "visitor" website because I live in the Loop and often encounter tourists in my day-to-day life. I am also very interested in Chicago history.

My family has been in Chicago for several generations. In fact, I decended from people who owned and managed a Boarding House that sat just north of the Chicago River behind what is now the Reid-Murdoch Building. They were displaced by the Great Chicago Fire of 1871.
